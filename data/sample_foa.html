NSF 26-101: AI and Climate Resilience for Undergraduate Research

Posted Date: January 15, 2026

Application Deadline: April 30, 2026

Funding Opportunity Number: NSF 26-101

Eligibility: Eligible applicants include accredited U.S. institutions of higher education, minority-serving institutions, and organizations supporting underserved communities and undergraduate students.

Program Description: This program supports interdisciplinary, community-engaged projects that apply machine learning, data science, and predictive modeling to climate adaptation and public health resilience. Proposals should include pilot experiments, stakeholder co-design, and evidence-based implementation plans. Award range is $150,000 to $750,000.
